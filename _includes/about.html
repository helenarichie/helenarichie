SIA

STEPUP Image Analysis (SIA) is a tool for easy photometric analysis that I devloped in the summer of 2017 in Python. Though it was originally intended specficially for STEPUP's use to process photometric data of light curves, it has since been expanded for general astronomical use and is being developed for implementation at the Allgheny Observatory. Check out SIA's GitHub to download and view documentation and user instructions!

STEPUP

I have been the lead undergraduate of the Survey of Transiting Extrasolar Planets at the University of Pittsburgh (STEPUP) since the summer of 2017. STEPUP is an undergraduate research group that uses transit photometry to detect exoplanets, conducting observations using the 16" Keeler Telescope based out of the Allegheny Observatory. Recently, STEPUP has shifted its focus to observing for the TESS Follow-up Observing Program Sub-Group 1 (TFOP SG1) in order to vet new TESS detections for false positives.
